Story

“Mantar” is a story in 20 parts. You just read 12 of them.

“Mantar” was written by Cem Eskinazi and Nic Schumann in the spring of 2021. The story was loosely inspired by the design of the typeface.

This website and associated tools for non-linear narrative were designed and built by Nic Schumann.

Typeface

“Mantar” is a font designed by Cem Eskinazi and released by Occupant Fonts in 2021. It consists of 12 styles across Roman and Italic, and supports Western European character sets.

Additional support for the development of “Mantar” was provided by Cyrus Highsmith, Marie Otsuka, and June Shin.

Press “Esc” at any time for an overview of the character set. “Mantar” is available for sale at TypeNetwork.
